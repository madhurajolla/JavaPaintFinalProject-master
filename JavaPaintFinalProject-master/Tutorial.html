JPAINT APPLICATION

 

CHOOSE SHAPE

Here you can choose desired type for shape from the dropdown to draw on the paint canvas ex: Rectangle, Droplet, Triangle etc... 


CHOOSE PRIMARY COLOR

Here you can select the color from the dropdown to draw your shapes on the paint canvas, primary color is used when the shape is filled in, outline and outline and filled in.

CHOOSE SECONDARY COLOR

Here you can select the color for the outline of the shapes from the dropdown to draw your shapes on the paint canvas.

CHOOSE SHADING TYPE

Here you will be having three types Filled In, Outline, Outline and filled in where you can draw with different colors.

CHOOSE MOUSE MODE

Here you will be having three options. 

  • Draw: Used to draw the shapes.
  • Select: Used to select the shapes to perform the required operations.
  • Move: Used to move the shapes.

 

UNDO

Here you can undo the previous operation performed.

REDO 

Here you can redo the previous operation performed.

COPY 

Here you can copy the required shape after the selecting the shape.

PASTE 

Here you can paste the copied shape.

DELETE 

Here you can delete the selected shape.

GROUP

Here you can group multiple shapes and perform the operations like Copy, Paste, Delete, Undo, and Redo.

UNGROUP

Here you can ungroup the grouped shapes.
